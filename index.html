Integral Lens

See the world in a new way.

There’s a theory that says that every point of view has a partial truth, and integrating them all together helps us see the bigger picture. All the parts of a whole fit together for a higher purpose. This is the essence of Ken Wilber’s integral approach. Put on an integral lens and see for yourself!

Quadrants

Everything has an internal and an external face, and everything shows up both individually and collectively, all happening at the same time. Want to see?

Levels

Everything grows, typically following a universal pattern where each stage transcends and includes the previous, ideally integrating the best from the foundational earlier levels. See it in action?

States

Humans have access to different states of consciousness, temporary configurations of reality that facilitate a very different experience of the world around us. See more.
